Academic Programs

Comprehensive Education in Jain Philosophy, Culture, and Literature

Programs Offered

The Centre will offer a range of Short Term, Certificate, Diploma, and Degree Programmes. Our curriculum is designed to provide a deep and comprehensive understanding of Jain studies.

Program Categories

Short Term Programs

Intensive courses ranging from 1-3 months focusing on specific aspects of Jain philosophy and culture.

  • Jain Ethics & Values
  • Jain Yoga & Meditation
  • Jain Literature Appreciation
  • Ahimsa in Modern Context

Certificate Programs

6-month to 1-year programs providing foundational knowledge in Jain studies and philosophy.

  • Foundation in Jain Philosophy
  • Jain History & Culture
  • Jain Scriptures Study
  • Comparative Religion Studies

Diploma Programs

1-2 year comprehensive programs offering in-depth study of Jain philosophy and its applications.

  • Advanced Jain Philosophy
  • Jain Literature & Languages
  • Jain Art & Architecture
  • Jain Ethics in Business

Degree Programs

3-4 year undergraduate and postgraduate programs for comprehensive academic study of Jainism.

  • Bachelor in Jain Studies
  • Master in Jain Philosophy
  • PhD in Jain Studies
  • Interdisciplinary Research

Curriculum Highlights

Core Subjects

Jain Philosophy

Study of fundamental principles, metaphysics, and ethical teachings of Jainism.

Jain Literature

Comprehensive study of Agamas, commentaries, and classical Jain texts.

Jain History & Culture

Historical development and cultural impact of Jainism in India and globally.

Jain Ethics & Values

Practical application of Jain principles in modern life and society.

Elective Subjects

Jain Yoga & Meditation

Practical training in Jain meditation techniques and spiritual practices.

Jain Art & Architecture

Study of Jain temples, sculptures, and artistic traditions.

Comparative Religion

Comparative study of Jainism with other world religions and philosophies.

Jain Languages

Learning Prakrit, Sanskrit, and other languages used in Jain literature.

Learning Methodology

Theoretical Learning

Comprehensive classroom lectures, seminars, and workshops on Jain philosophy and literature.

Practical Application

Hands-on experience through field visits, meditation sessions, and community engagement activities.

Research Projects

Independent research projects and thesis work to develop critical thinking and analytical skills.
