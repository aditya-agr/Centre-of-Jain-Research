Admission Information

Join Our Journey in Jain Studies and Academic Excellence

Admission Process

How to Apply

1

Application Submission

Submit your application form along with required documents through our online portal or in person at the Centre office.

2

Document Verification

Our team will verify your academic credentials, identity documents, and supporting materials.

3

Interview Process

Selected candidates will be called for an interview to assess their interest, aptitude, and commitment to Jain studies.

4

Final Selection

Merit-based selection and admission confirmation will be communicated to successful candidates via email and phone.

Admission Requirements

Educational Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Proficiency in English/Hindi
  • Basic knowledge of Jain philosophy preferred

Required Documents

  • Academic transcripts
  • Identity proof (Aadhar/PAN)
  • Passport size photographs
  • Statement of purpose
  • Recommendation letters (2)

Application Process

  • Online application form
  • Application fee payment
  • Document upload
  • Application review
  • Interview scheduling
