About the Seminar

Jainism, one of the most ancient traditions of India, has profoundly enriched the Indian Knowledge System (IKS) through its philosophical depth, ethical teachings, literature, art, and scientific approach to life. The lineage of Jain Acharyas contributed significantly in the various fields of Indian Literature and played a vital role in the upliftment of Indian Society and spreading moral values. This seminar on “Jainism and Indian Knowledge System” aims to explore the contribution of Jain knowledge and culture in shaping the intellectual, social and cultural traditions of India, and its contemporary relevance in building a sustainable, peaceful, and inclusive society.

About Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya

Established in 1964 as the University of Indore, and later renamed in honor of the great Holkar queen 'Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar', DAVV is a premier state university in Central India. Accredited with an 'A+' grade by NAAC, it stands as a beacon of higher education, research, and innovation. With a sprawling campus and numerous affiliated colleges, the university offers a vast array of programs across multiple disciplines including Technology, Management, Sciences, and Humanities. It is dedicated to fostering an environment of academic excellence and preparing students to become global leaders and socially responsible citizens.

About The Centre for Jain Studies

The Centre for Jain Studies, established under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK), is a nodal centre for academic excellence in Jain philosophy, culture, and literature. Our mission is to advance the study, preservation, and promotion of Jain heritage as an integral part of the Indian Knowledge System. Through rigorous teaching, interdisciplinary research, publications, and community engagement, we seek to inspire individuals and societies to embrace the timeless values of non-violence (Ahimsa), compassion, and sustainability for a peaceful and harmonious world.

Seminar Sub-Themes

Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences

  • Values, Ethics, and Logic in Jain Philosophy
  • Jain Contributions to Culture, Languages, and Literature
  • Psychology, Economics, and Commerce from a Jain Perspective
  • The Role of Yoga and Spiritual Traditions in Jainism
  • Grammar, Art, and Lifestyle in Jain Heritage

Science, Technology & Mathematics

  • Jain Concepts in Astronomy, Cosmology, and Astrology
  • Archeology and Design in Jain Architecture
  • Mathematical Principles in Ancient Jain Texts
  • Ayurveda, Agriculture, and Food Sciences in Jainism
  • Insights into Physics, Biology, and Environmental Science
