About The Centre for Jain Studies

The Centre for Jain Studies has been established at Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore, under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK), to provide a dedicated academic platform for the study, research, and dissemination of Jain philosophy, culture, and literature. We aim to advance the preservation and promotion of Jain heritage as an integral part of Indian knowledge traditions, inspiring society to embrace values of non-violence, compassion, and sustainability.

Preserve Jain Heritage

Offering academic programs, publishing research, and hosting events to promote the rich heritage and understanding of Jainism.

Contribute to Indian Knowledge

Researching the contribution of Jain Literature in fields like art, philosophy, science, and mathematics.

Foster Interdisciplinary Research

Exploring Jainism's multifaceted contributions and the scientific validation of its practices like Ahimsa and Aahar.

Enhance Global Understanding

Collaborating with scholars and institutions worldwide to disseminate knowledge about Jainism globally.

Support Community Engagement

Serving as a hub for dialogue between scholars, practitioners, and the general public.

Message from the Director

Prof. Rajnish Jain

Bachelor of Science (BSC), Ph.D. (Management), M.B.A.

"We are committed to creating a vibrant academic environment where the profound principles of Jainism are explored with scholarly rigor and contemporary relevance."

With over 36 years of academic experience in teaching, research, and institution building at Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, and five years in national policy making as the Secretary of the University Grants Commission (UGC), I am honored to lead this Centre. Our vision is to build a hub of excellence that not only preserves ancient wisdom but also demonstrates its application in solving modern challenges. We invite students, scholars, and the community to join us in this enlightening journey.
