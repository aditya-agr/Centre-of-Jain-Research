About The Centre for Jain Studies

The Centre for Jain Studies has been established at Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore, under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK), to provide a dedicated academic platform for the study, research, and dissemination of Jain philosophy, culture, and literature. We aim to advance the preservation and promotion of Jain heritage as an integral part of Indian knowledge traditions, inspiring society to embrace values of non-violence, compassion, and sustainability.

Preserve Jain Heritage

Offering academic programs, publishing research, and hosting events to promote the rich heritage and understanding of Jainism.

Contribute to Indian Knowledge

Researching the contribution of Jain Literature in fields like art, philosophy, science, and mathematics.

Foster Interdisciplinary Research

Exploring Jainism's multifaceted contributions and the scientific validation of its practices like Ahimsa and Aahar.

Enhance Global Understanding

Collaborating with scholars and institutions worldwide to disseminate knowledge about Jainism globally.

Support Community Engagement

Serving as a hub for dialogue between scholars, practitioners, and the general public.

Our Vision

The Centre for Jain Studies envisions becoming a leading national and international hub for academic research and dissemination of Jain philosophy. It shall strive to nurture scholars and practitioners who embody the principles of non-violence, compassion, and harmony, thereby contributing to the creation of a peaceful and sustainable society. It will also serve as a nodal centre for various academic, cultural and social activities in line with Jain traditions and heritage.

Our Objectives

Promote Advanced Studies

To promote teaching, research, and advanced studies in Jainism, philosophy, culture, history, literature, Jain Yoga and allied subjects.

Preserve Knowledge Resources

To preserve, document and enhance Knowledge Resources on Jainism for academic and cultural enrichment.

Study Jain Literature

To study the contribution of Jain Literature in different domains of the Indian Knowledge System.

Develop Interdisciplinary Linkages

To connect Jain philosophy with modern fields such as ethics, ecology, peace studies, management, and wellness.

Provide Vocational Training

To provide Vocational Training in Lifestyle, Health, Craft, Yoga, and other skills.

Collaborate Globally

To collaborate with national and global scholars, centres, and institutions of Jain Studies and Interdisciplinary areas.

Organize Community Outreach

To organize outreach activities and publish scholarly articles, newsletters, journals, and books.

Provide International Platform

To provide an international platform for the advanced studies of Jainism in various contexts.

Conduct Seminars & Workshops

To conduct seminars, workshops, and conferences for spreading awareness about Jain values, philosophy, and their practical applications.

Promote Jain Yoga & Meditation

To promote practices of Jain Yoga, meditation, and mindfulness for holistic development and well-being.
