Research & Publications

Advancing Knowledge Through Scholarly Research and Academic Excellence

Research Activities

Academic Research

Conducting interdisciplinary research on Jain philosophy, literature, and its contributions to Indian Knowledge System.

Publications

Publishing scholarly articles, journals, and books on Jain studies and their contemporary relevance.

Collaborations

Building partnerships with national and international institutions for collaborative research projects.
