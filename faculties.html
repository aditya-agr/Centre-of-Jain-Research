Our Faculty

Distinguished Leadership and Academic Excellence

Messages from Leadership

Prof. Rakesh Singhai

Vice Chancellor, Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya

"It is a matter of great pride that Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya is establishing the Centre for Jain Studies with the support of the Ministry of Minority Affairs. This Centre will serve as a national hub for preserving Jain heritage and advancing scholarly research. I am confident that it will significantly contribute to the enrichment of the Indian Knowledge System and promote values of peace, harmony, and non-violence."

Prof. Rajnish Jain

Director, Centre for Jain Studies

"The Centre for Jain Studies is envisioned as a space where academic excellence, cultural preservation, and community engagement come together. With dedicated programs, research initiatives, and outreach activities, the Centre aims to highlight the timeless wisdom of Jain philosophy and its relevance in the modern world. We look forward to building collaborations at national and international levels."

Prof. Rahul Singhai

Deputy Director

"Our goal is to foster an inclusive space for deep inquiry, where ancient Jain wisdom informs and enriches modern academic disciplines, creating a new generation of scholars."

As an active researcher and educator, I am excited to contribute to the Centre's mission. We aim to facilitate interdisciplinary research, validate ancient practices with scientific rigor, and engage the community through meaningful dialogue. We believe this Centre will be a beacon for those seeking to understand and apply Jain principles in today's world.
